Welcome to HackRice!

HackRice is Rice University's official Hackathon!

We're hosting Rice's second ever hackathon! Build creative and useful apps by taking advantage of web services and developer tools offered by various websites. The contest is free form meaining you can make any type of mobile, desktop, or web app you want. Participants will demonstrate their application to a panel of judges, and prizes will be awarded for various categories.
