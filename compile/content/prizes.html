Judging and Prizes

Teams will have an opportunity to present their applications on Sunday evening to the audience and a panel of 2 judges.

We will not judge code quality or style. However, it is recommended that you write clear and readable code to enable your team to work more efficiently.

Applications will be judged for the following category prizes. Teams may win across multiple categories. For each category, there will be a 1st place prize of $100 per team member and a runner-up prize of $50 per team member. Prizes will be awarded after the event in the form of a prepaid card.

Prize Amounts

1st Place: $100 per team member per category

Runner-up: $50 per team member per category

Category Prizes

Most Rice Applicable - Awarded to the team whose whose application would be found most useful by people at Rice.

Most User Friendly - Awarded to the team whose application provides the most intuitive and appealing user experience.

Most Useful - Awarded to the team whose application would be the most useful to a consumer, organization or business person.
