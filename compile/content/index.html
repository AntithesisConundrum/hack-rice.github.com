Welcome to HackRice!

HackRice is Rice University's official Hackathon!

We're hosting a collaborative computer programming competition. Build creative and useful apps by taking advantage of web services and developer tools offered by various websites. You can make any type of mobile or web app you want. Participants will demonstrate their application to a panel of judges, and prizes will be awarded various categories.
