Judging and Prizes

Teams will have an opportunity to present their applications on Sunday evening to the audience and a panel of judges representing sponsors as well as Rice professors.

We will not judge code quality or style. However, it is recommended that you write clear and readable code to enable your team to work more efficiently.

1st, 2nd, and 3rd places will be judged by UI, funtionality, and utility.
