Traveling to HackRice

If you've been accepted to HackRice from out of Rice, here is what you need to know about planning your travel. We're very excited to see you here!

*Note that all reimbursements are for round trips only.

Logistics

Date + Time

You should look to arrive at Rice at 6:00 PM, January 15th for check-in. The hackathon ends at 3:00 PM, January 17th, so please keep that in mind when scheduling your return flights.

Getting Here

Fly in to either IAH or HOU. You can then take Bus 73 and transfer onto the MetroRail at Fannin South to get to Rice from HOU, or Bus 102 and transfer onto the MetroRail at Downtown Transit Center to get to Rice from IAH. Alternatively, Ubers are available and will cost about $30.

If you plan on being reimbursed, you will need to bring a physical copy of your receipt or email us a scanned copy before January 31st.

HackRice is located at Rice Memorial Center, Rice University, Houston, TX (also known as the Ley Student Center or RMC). Rice's address is 6100 Main Street, Houston, TX 77005.

Buses

HackRice Buses

We will be sending two buses: one to UT Austin , and the other to UT Dallas / Texas A & M.

The UT Austin bus will stop at Littlefield Fountain (W 21st St, Austin, TX 78705). It will leave at 3:00PM.

The UTD/TAMU bus will stop at University of Texas, Dallas (800 W Campbell Rd, Richardson, TX 75080) and leave at 12:30PM. It will then stop at Wisenbaker Building (88 Bizzell St, College Station, TX 77840) and leave at 4:00PM.

Megabus

If you choose to take a Megabus, then we can reimburse up to $20 of your fare.

Driving + Parking

If you are driving, we will reimburse up to $50 per team, so we encourage you to carpool together with your entire team. Parking is available at Greenbriar Lot, behind the stadium, for a dollar a day. There will be a bus to take you on to main campus. Check out Rice's campus map .
